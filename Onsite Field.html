Onsite field

Planting

Help improve the environment by planting and make communities greener, cleaner, and healthier. help fight climate change while creating beautiful and sustainable spaces

Animal Care

Give animals a better life by assisting with their care, supporting rescue efforts, and helping them find loving homes.

Hospital Visits

Bring comfort and support to patients by visiting them in hospitals, offering companionship, listening to their needs, and helping them feel less alone during their recovery.

Event Organising

Help plan and run events by coordinating activities, managing logistics, and supporting teams , setting up venues, and ensuring the event is successful, enjoyable, and well-organized.

Elderly Support

Assisting older adults through companionship, daily support, social activities, and programs that improve their quality of life.

Fundraising & Donations

Help raise funds and collect donations to support charitable projects, community programs, and nonprofit organizations through campaigns, events, and donor outreach

Clean-Up Campaigns

Participate in activities that help keep communities clean and healthy, such as collecting litter, cleaning public spaces, beaches, parks, and promoting environmental awareness.

Refugee Teaching

Support refugees by teaching language, literacy, academic subjects, or practical skills that help them integrate into their new communities and improve their opportunities.

First Aid Help

Provide basic emergency medical assistance to injured or sick people until professional medical care arrives, including treating minor injuries and supporting emergency response teams.

Disaster Intervention

Help during emergencies like floods, earthquakes, or fires by assisting with rescue support, distributing relief supplies, and helping affected communities recover safely and quickly.

Homeless Support

Assist people experiencing homelessness by providing food, clothing, shelter support, essential services, and compassionate community assistance

Disability Care

Support people with disabilities by helping with daily activities, accessibility needs, mobility, social inclusion, and providing companionship and assistance in community programs.

Support Groups

Help people facing similar challenges by providing emotional support, sharing experiences, and creating a safe space for discussion, encouragement, and mutual understanding.

Mosque Care

Support the maintenance and cleanliness of mosques, assist with organizing community activities, and help ensure a welcoming and respectful environment for worshippers.

Historical Heritage Preservation

Help protect and maintain historical sites, monuments, and cultural artifacts through restoration work, awareness campaigns, and supporting museums or heritage organizations

Sports & Recreation

Supporting sports teams, coaching, organizing recreational activities, and encouraging healthy lifestyles.
