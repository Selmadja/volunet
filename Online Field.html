online field


Translation

Help translate written or spoken content between languages so people and organizations can communicate clearly and access important information.

Writing

Create and edit content such as articles, reports, stories, social media posts, or educational materials to support organizations and spread information.

Graphic Design

Create visual content like posters, logos, flyers, and social media graphics to help organizations communicate messages clearly and attract attention

Coding

Use programming skills to build websites, apps, tools, or software that help organizations solve problems, improve services, and manage information.

Online Teaching

Teach subjects or skills through the internet by giving lessons, tutoring students, or creating educational content to support learners remotely

Editing

Review and improve written, audio, or video content by correcting mistakes, enhancing clarity, and making the final material more professional and effective.

Voice Over

Record spoken audio for videos, ads, lessons, or announcements to help organizations communicate messages clearly and engagingly.

Online Advocacy

Use digital platforms like social media, blogs, and campaigns to raise awareness, promote causes, and encourage positive social or environmental change.

Virtual Events

Help organize and support online events such as webinars, workshops, and meetings by managing platforms, assisting participants, and ensuring smooth communication.

Support Calls

Provide help and emotional support to people over the phone by listening, giving guidance, and connecting them with useful services or resources.

Virtual Admin Support

Help organizations remotely by managing emails, scheduling, data entry, document organization, and other administrative tasks to keep operations running smoothly.

Fundraising Online Campaigns

Help organizations raise money by creating, promoting, and managing online donation campaigns and crowdfunding efforts through social media and digital platforms.
